## 🔥What is the Design System?

A design system is a collection of **_reusable components_**, guided by **_clear standards_**, that can be **_assembled together to build any number of applications_**.

- Invision blog

---

## Why use a design system?

- comply with the latest federal policy for websites
- accessibility out of the box
- easy to adapt
- **reuse, reuse, reuse**
